How to Play Mathle

Mathle is a puzzle game inspired by Wordle. Your goal is to guess a valid equation by filling in numbers and operators!

Both sides of the equation MUST be equal!

For example:
3+5=8 is valid
3+5=9 is not

How To Play

You have 6 attempts to guess the equation. Each guess must be a valid mathematical equation where:

  • The left side equals the right side
  • All cells are filled
  • The order of operations is followed

After each guess, the colors of the cells change to show how close you are:

Green: Correct number/operator in the correct position
Purple: Correct number/operator but wrong position
Gray: Not in the equation at all

Game Modes

Daily Challenge: Play the same puzzle as everyone else!
Complete it once per day and build your streak.
Custom settings do not apply!

Custom Game: Create your own puzzle with customizable settings:

  • Choose grid size
  • Select operators to use (+, -, *, รท, ^)
  • Play unlimited games with your preferred difficulty

Stat Tracking

Your performance is automatically saved:

  • Games played
  • Wins and losses
  • Distribution of wins by attempt number
  • Daily challenge completion rate
  • Current streak

Additional Rules

  • Operators are always on the left side of the equation
  • The answer can be negative
  • PEMDAS applies
  • 0 can't appear on its own or before larger numbers (07 + 0 = 7)
